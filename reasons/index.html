Little Things & Reasons

There are countless reasons Batman loves My Moonlight. Here are just a few:

  • Her laugh fills the room with light and makes every worry fade.
  • She listens with her whole heart and understands without judgement.
  • She believes in Batman’s dreams and encourages him to pursue them.
  • Her kindness to strangers reminds Batman of the goodness in the world.
  • The way she says “we” when talking about the future.
  • The way she whispers “I’m here” when Batman feels lost.

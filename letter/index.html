A Letter I Never Fully Said

You are the knife I turn inside myself; that is love. That, my dear, is love. — Franz Kafka【894360731246386†L149-L153】

My dearest My Moonlight,

I often find myself searching for the perfect words to tell you how much you mean to me. You turn my dark nights into mornings full of hope. Your presence calms my storms and amplifies my joy. Loving you has taught me patience, vulnerability, and the beauty of growing together.

I will never forget the way you ran into my arms at midnight on my birthday, still breathless from your high‑priority task, eyes sparkling. That moment was a gift in itself, just like the moon lamp you gave me to gaze upon whenever I miss you and your handwritten letter that I keep folded near my heart.

Thank you for choosing me, for trusting me, and for standing beside me every time life became uncertain. Thank you for the laughter, the hugs, the late‑night calls, and the simple acts of love. The future looks bright because you're in it, and I look forward to making a million more memories with you.

Always and forever,
Batman

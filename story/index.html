How It All Started

Batman still remembers the first time he saw My Moonlight laugh. That moment on 14 Feb sparked a connection that quickly became the centre of their world.

Their early chats were full of curiosity and warmth. They talked about everything from favourite movies to life goals, discovering an uncanny alignment in the things that mattered most. They soon found themselves spending hours on the phone, sharing secrets and building trust.

On one unforgettable evening, Batman confessed his feelings, afraid yet hopeful. My Moonlight smiled and told him she felt the same. From that moment, their story began in earnest — a journey filled with love, laughter and growth. Later, she surprised him with a
moon‑shaped lamp to brighten his nights whenever he missed her, and a handwritten love letter that he reads whenever he needs warmth.

I want in fact more of you. In my mind I am dressing you with light; I am wrapping you up in blankets of complete acceptance and then I give myself to you. I long for you; I who usually long without longing, as though I am unconscious and absorbed in neutrality and apathy, really, utterly long for every bit of you. — Franz Kafka【404931808697548†L63-L67】
